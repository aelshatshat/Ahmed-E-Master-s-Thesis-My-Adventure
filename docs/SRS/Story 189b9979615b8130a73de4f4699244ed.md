# Story

---

# Plot Summary

You are Ahmed. Evil beings plague the lands. With your sword and magic, you shall slay them; or, you will die trying.

# Story Progression

Killing bosses alters the state of the world.

The Celestial Primate of Wisdom is meant to be the first boss.

## Protagonist(s)

Ahmed

## Antagonists(s)

- Celestial Primate of Wisdom (Wizard Orangutan)
    - Moves:
        - Lightning Bolt - Shoots a volley of lightning bolts from his finger towards the Player Avatar
        - Meteor Swarm - Shoots many small meteors that track the Player Avatar for a short duration
        - Flame Columns - Summons pillars of flame across the floor for a short duration
        - Cold Shock - Slowly freeze an area that deals damage after a short duration, and slows the Player Avatar if it takes damage from it
        - MID COMBAT EVENT: Chaos Crush
            - Can avoid damage by idling in the middle of the screen (Yin Yang Defense)
            - Can also try and avoid the collapsing space that appears in the middle of the screen

# Setting & Worldbuilding

- Each boss has an arena
