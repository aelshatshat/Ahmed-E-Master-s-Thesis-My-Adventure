### High-level Details

**Brief Story: You are me (Ahmed). You are on an adventure to slay evil bosses and freaks and such.**

**Main Objective: Kill all bosses as quickly as you possible can. Don’t get killed.**

**Gameplay Pillars:** 

- Fast-paced, skill-based combat
- Quick movement and platforming mechanics
- Progressively unfolding story elements
- Boss-focused gameplay with unique, challenging encounters
